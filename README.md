# DRIVEWAY

**Survive until 3:00 PM.**

A browser game where you protect your driveway from school pickup parents. 
Click cars to hit them with paint before they block you in.

## Play

Open `index.html` in any browser — no install, no dependencies.

Or play online: https://anjaleeDS.github.io/Driveway/

## How to Play

- **Click a car** to hit it with paint (1–3 hits to send them away)
- **Shoot approaching cars** before they park to turn them back entirely
- **Place tools** (keys 1–3) to slow cars down:
  - 🚧 Cone — slows a car for ~5.5s (max 3)
  - 🗑️ Trash — longer delay ~8s, COACH MIKE ignores it (max 1)
  - 🪧 Sign — they read it, park anyway (max 1)
- **Sanity** drains while your driveway is blocked — hit zero and it's game over
- Survive all 5 days (Mon–Fri) to win

## Tips

- The last 10 minutes before 3 PM get chaotic — listen for the bell at 2:50
- Sanity drains faster as the week goes on — Friday is brutal
- Heavy vehicles (CHAD, COACH MIKE, MIKE SR.) take more hits and ignore some tools

## Built with

Single HTML file — Canvas 2D, Web Audio API, zero dependencies.
